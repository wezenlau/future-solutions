Our Fundraising Campaign

The distance between Nairobi, Kenya and Loruko Primary School is approximately 300 miles. We are going to cycle, run and travel the equivalent distance without using any electrical or powered means to raise funds for our project. The distance will be split between five of us, which amounts to around 60 miles each.
