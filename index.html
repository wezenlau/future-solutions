About Us

We are students of UTC Reading, developing a solution to Loruko Primary School in Karare, Kenya. We are extremely passionate about aiding these children who are living in rural conditions, where water and basic school supplies are still a dream. Partnered with the Amuka Foundation, we have never been presented with a more tremendous opportunity to make a real difference. Your help is crucial for the effective implementation of our plans, so please consider donating to support the struggling children.
